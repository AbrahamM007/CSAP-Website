About CSAP

CSAP is a nonprofit, student-led high school outreach program dedicated to promoting achievement in core computer science concepts through civic engagement.

We work alongside community tech non-profits and teach students in a hands-on, interactive environment.

Programs

Teaching over 25 LAUSD students as part of summer sessions with interactive lessons emphasizing 'learn by doing'.

Guest Speakers

We collaborate with industry leaders from companies like RIOT Games and NVIDIA to provide valuable insights and mentorship.

Annual CSAP Competition

Every year, students compete to become CSAP Scholars by solving community issues using newly learned software concepts.
