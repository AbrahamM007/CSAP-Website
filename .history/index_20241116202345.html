About CSAP

CSAP is a nonprofit, student-led high school outreach program dedicated to serving students of all backgrounds through achievement in core computer science concepts. Our program promotes civic engagement and hands-on learning to inspire the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Programs

Intro to Web Development

Hands-on lessons that focus on practical coding skills, paced to suit various learning styles.

Workshops & Projects

Weekly workshops that encourage exploration and 'learning by doing' to solidify coding concepts.

Guest Speakers

Gain insights from industry leaders who share their journeys and offer guidance to our students. Past speakers include experts from RIOT Games, NVIDIA, and more.

Annual CSAP Competition

Showcase your skills and creativity in our annual competition, where schools under our program tackle important community issues using software solutions. Compete for the title of CSAP Scholar and make a real-world impact.
